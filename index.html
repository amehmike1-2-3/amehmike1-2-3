📜 Rules & Regulations

NeyoMarket is built on trust, transparency, and fairness. Please read carefully.

🛡️

Seller Verification

All sellers must verify their identity and bank account via BVN or NIN before listing products for sale.

🚫

Prohibited Items

Selling counterfeit goods, stolen items, illegal software, weapons, or any item prohibited by Nigerian law is strictly banned.

📦

Accurate Descriptions

All product listings must accurately describe the item. Misleading titles, descriptions, or images will result in removal.

💳

Payment Protection

All payments are processed securely through Paystack. Sellers receive 90% of the sale price. 10% is retained as platform fee.

🔄

Refund Policy

Buyers may request refunds within 3 days of purchase for physical goods and within 24 hours for digital goods if not as described.

⚖️

Dispute Resolution

Disputes are reviewed by our team within 48 hours. Both parties must provide evidence. Our decision is final.

👤

Account Conduct

Harassment, impersonation, fake reviews, or any abusive behavior will result in immediate and permanent account suspension.

💸

Split Payments

NeyoMarket uses Paystack split payments. 90% goes to sellers, 10% to the platform to fund operations, support, and growth.

🔐

Data Privacy

We never share your personal data with third parties without consent. All data is stored securely in compliance with NDPR.

📣

Affiliate Conduct

Affiliates may not use spam, misleading ads, or create fake accounts. Self-purchases via own affiliate links are void.

📊

Digital Delivery

Digital products must be delivered within 24 hours of purchase. Sellers are responsible for ensuring download links are working.

🏷️

Pricing Integrity

Artificial price inflation, fee manipulation, or any scheme to circumvent the platform fee structure is prohibited.

Violation Consequences

First violation: Warning + 7-day suspension. Second violation: Permanent ban + fund freeze pending review. Serious violations (fraud, illegal items) reported to relevant authorities.
