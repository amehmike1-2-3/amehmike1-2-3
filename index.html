📜 Rules & Regulations

NeyoMarket is built on trust and fairness. These rules protect everyone.

🛡️

Seller Verification

All sellers must verify identity (NIN/BVN) and bank account before listing. No exceptions.

🚫

Prohibited Items

Counterfeit goods, stolen items, pirated software, weapons, illegal substances — immediate ban + EFCC report.

📦

Accurate Listings

Product names, descriptions, images must be truthful. Fake listings are removed and sellers are suspended.

💳

Escrow Payments

All payments go through Paystack escrow. Funds are only released after buyer confirms receipt. 10% platform fee applies.

🔄

Refund Policy

Physical goods: 3-day refund window. Digital goods: 24hr refund if not as described. Buyer must provide evidence.

⚖️

Dispute Resolution

Disputes reviewed within 24 hours. Both parties submit evidence. Admin decision is final and binding.

👤

Account Conduct

Harassment, fake reviews, impersonation, and abusive behaviour = immediate permanent ban.

💸

Split Payments

90% of every sale goes to the seller. 10% is the NeyoMarket platform fee. Affiliates paid from seller commission.

🔐

Data Privacy

We never sell your data. All data stored securely. NDPR compliant. Emails are masked in all public-facing UI.

📣

Affiliate Conduct

No spam. No misleading promotions. No self-referrals. Violations result in commission reversal and ban.

📊

Digital Delivery

Digital products must deliver within 24hrs. Download links must be working. Log timestamps are recorded.

🏷️

Pricing Integrity

Artificial inflation or fee circumvention schemes are prohibited. Price must match the delivered value.

⚠ Violation Consequences

First offence: Warning + 7-day suspension. Second offence: Permanent ban + fund freeze. Fraud or illegal listings: EFCC report + legal action. No appeals for fraud cases.

1. Escrow System
All payments are held in escrow via Paystack until the buyer confirms receipt. Funds are released to the seller after confirmation.

Sellers must deliver goods as described. Misrepresentation results in instant refund to buyer and seller suspension.

Buyers may dispute any order within 3 days (physical) or 24 hours (digital) if goods are not as described.
